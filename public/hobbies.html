Hobbies

Gardening

I love to grow vegetables and flowers. I like to start my vegetable seeds indoors around April. I would start earlier but our weather is not warm enough. I like to grow vegetables I can later can. I like to make jellies also but I have to buy my fruits from local fruit stands for that.

I have many varieties of flowers around my home. I like to plant them so that I can see the different blooms and colors throughout the seasons. My flowers begin blooming in March and finish around October.

Crafts

Crafting is a great stress reliever for me. I enjoy many different types of crafts. I like to crochet small projects that have intricate patterns.

I have also done some jewelry making. I have used wire and jems to create beautiful necklaces, rings and bracelets. I had an Etsy shop in the past where I would sell my pieces.

I got into paper planners a few years ago. This is like scrapbooking but with stickers instead of photos. I found I was spending quite a bit of money buying the stickers so I decided to make my own. I did quite a bit of research and taught myself how to use the silhouette design studio software. I sold some of my stickers as digital downloads through my Etsy shop. Now I offer free sticker sets through my blog.

Technology

I love to learn how to use new technology. As mentioned above I taught myself to use the Silhouette design studio software. I also learned how to use the Cricut design space. I have a Facebook group where I offer my planner stickers for free and I am always helping others learn how to access those files.

Now that I am an empty nester I have decided to learn web development. With the help of the Udemy Web Development Bootcamp I hope to soon be able to develop websites and other projects.
